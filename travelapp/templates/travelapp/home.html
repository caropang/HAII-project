How it Works

TravelMatch won't provide a specific list of things to do (see TripAdvisor), but it can provide a starting point for thinking about activites to research for your next trip!

This tool recommends and ranks 23 broad categories of activities which can help you discover new travel interests.

We use a machine learning model to make these recommendations. We use data which was originally collected from Google reviews, featuring over 5000 users and their average ratings on attractions from 23 different categories across Europe. This was sourced from the UCI Machine Learning Repository .

We find users that share similar interests with you based on your questionaire answers, and then recommend activities that these users also liked!
